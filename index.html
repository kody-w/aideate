What is the AI Acceleration Program?

The Microsoft AI Acceleration Program is a hands-on engagement designed to help organizations move from AI curiosity to AI action. We work with your team to identify high-impact business challenges, assess AI readiness, and build a prioritized roadmap.

🎯

Identify

Surface real business pain points across departments — not hypothetical use cases.

📐

Assess

Evaluate each opportunity for impact, feasibility, data readiness, and ROI potential.

🗺️

Prioritize

Walk out with an executive-ready roadmap of quick wins and strategic AI investments.

What You'll Walk Away With
Prioritized list of AI opportunities ranked by impact
Business case with ROI estimates for top initiatives
Implementation roadmap with quick wins identified
Executive-ready report (Word, Excel, and PDF)
Clear next steps with owners and timelines
